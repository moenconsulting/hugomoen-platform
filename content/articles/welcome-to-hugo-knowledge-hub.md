## Why Build My Own Platform?

In a world where more and more content is published on borrowed platforms, I wanted a place where I own both the content and the context.

Hugo Knowledge Hub is the primary home for everything I write.

LinkedIn and other channels are used for distribution and conversation.

The content lives here.

The goal is not to produce as much content as possible.

The goal is to create a place for reflection, learning and knowledge sharing over time.

---

## What Can You Find Here?

The platform brings together my thoughts, experiences and observations on topics such as:

- Architecture
- Organization Design
- Product Development
- Leadership
- Strategy
- Decision-Making

Some articles are short reflections.

Others explore broader themes over time.

One example is the **Decision Architecture Framework**, an evolving framework that explores how organizations make, challenge and learn from decisions.

---

## Knowledge That Evolves

Not everything on this platform is finished.

Some articles represent concrete experiences.

Others are hypotheses that are still being explored.

Frameworks, observations and ideas will continue to evolve as new insights emerge.

The goal is not to appear as someone who has all the answers.

The goal is to learn openly.

---

## The Technology Behind It

The platform is built with:

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS

Content is stored as Markdown files.

No database.

No CMS.

Just content, code and version control.

As simple as possible.

---

## Welcome

If any of the content sparks new ideas, challenges existing perspectives or contributes to better decisions, then the platform has fulfilled its purpose.
